# Design Guidelines: Michelle Tianna Antiques

## Design Approach
**Reference-Based Luxury E-commerce**: Drawing inspiration from Sotheby's, Christie's, and high-end antique dealers. Emphasis on sophistication, heritage, and credibility with clean, elegant presentation.

## Typography Hierarchy

**Primary Font**: Playfair Display (serif) - for headings, conveys heritage and luxury
**Secondary Font**: Inter or Lato (sans-serif) - for body text, ensures readability

- Hero Headline: 4xl-6xl, serif, medium weight
- Section Headings: 3xl-4xl, serif, semibold
- Subsections: xl-2xl, serif, medium
- Body Text: base-lg, sans-serif, regular
- Captions/Meta: sm-base, sans-serif, light

## Layout System

**Spacing Units**: Tailwind units of 4, 6, 8, 12, 16, 20, 24 (e.g., p-8, mb-12, py-20)

**Container Strategy**:
- Max-width: 7xl for full sections
- Max-width: 4xl for text-heavy content
- Full-width for hero and gallery sections

## Page Structure

### 1. Hero Section (80vh minimum)
Large, elegant hero featuring a curated antique piece as background with subtle overlay. Business name in large serif typography, professional motto beneath. Soft vignette effect for depth.

### 2. About Michelle Tianna
Two-column layout (desktop): Left - professional B&W portrait (square format), Right - comprehensive bio with international experience, credentials, and business philosophy. Include subtle decorative border element.

### 3. International Presence
Elegant display showing countries served (Dubai, USA, Italy, Germany, France, Canada) with subtle icons or typography treatment. Single row on desktop, stacked on mobile.

### 4. Antiques Gallery
Masonry-style grid (3 columns desktop, 2 tablet, 1 mobile) showcasing antique pieces. Each image with subtle border, descriptive caption, and hover elevation effect. Include lightbox functionality for full-screen viewing.

### 5. Services Overview
Two-column cards: "Acquiring Antiques" and "Supplying Collections" with elegant icons and detailed descriptions of working with companies, museums, and malls.

### 6. Contact Section
Multi-column layout with clear hierarchy:
- Email (with "Personal Email" label)
- Texting: 0434654261 (specify texting/calls accepted)
- WhatsApp: 0489925104 (WhatsApp only - business purposes)
Location displayed: Redbank Plains, Queensland, Australia

## Component Library

**Navigation**: Fixed header with logo left, minimal menu items right (About, Gallery, Services, Contact)

**Buttons**: 
- Primary: Subtle border with elegant hover state
- Secondary: Text-based with underline animation
- On images: Frosted glass background with blur effect

**Cards**: Minimal borders, generous padding (p-8), subtle shadow on hover

**Gallery Items**: Clean borders, caption overlay on hover, smooth transitions

**Logo Integration**: Elegant, refined logo (to be designed) placed in header and footer

## Images

**Hero Image**: Feature a stunning antique piece (French Empire bronze or ornate clock) as full-width background with elegant overlay

**Profile Image**: Michelle Tianna's B&W portrait, square crop, professional presentation

**Gallery Images**: All provided antique images displayed in masonry grid:
- Chinese porcelain
- French Empire bronzes
- Ornate clocks
- Baroque sculptures
- Various collectibles

## Animations
Minimal and refined:
- Smooth scroll behavior
- Subtle fade-in for sections on scroll
- Gallery hover elevations
- No distracting animations

## Accessibility
- High contrast text on all backgrounds
- Clear focus states on interactive elements
- Semantic HTML structure
- Alt text for all antique images with descriptions

## Unique Design Elements
- Decorative corner flourishes on major sections (subtle, heritage-inspired)
- Elegant divider lines between sections
- Sophisticated footer with business credentials and trust indicators

This design conveys luxury, expertise, and international credibility while maintaining excellent usability for potential clients from museums, companies, and malls.
